Operation Epsilon

Epsilon is a modpack dedicated to ease of use and appeals to all audiences of Minecraft-modpack players!

Our goal is to provide a modpack that everyone can enjoy - whether it be factories, exploration, or combat!

Download the latest version of Epsilon here: v0.2.1
